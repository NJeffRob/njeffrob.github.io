Week 3 update: relatively disappointed this week. I had a lot of other obligations for work/committees so I was very tired and couldn't focus when drawing (on top of keeping up with Ukulele practice/arranging and other projects I have on the go). I'm also getting to a point where I might need to break up drawings over several days to do more complicated ones; will play around with that next week.
