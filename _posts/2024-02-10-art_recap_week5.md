I've continued the trend of doing larger pieces that will take longer, but have run into another issue: pieces that would normally take me a good amount of time are taking me less time. The issue with that is that I don't necessarily think I'm getting better as much as I am getting impatient. Next week, I want to choose a single piece and spend the whole week REALLY making a good piece of art. Hopefully that ends up challenging me a bunch. Otherwise, I am relatively pleased with the pieces I've done this week. A friend of mine asked if I had considered doing colour, so that is another direction I might want to go in the near future (although I'd have to buy colour pencils lol).
