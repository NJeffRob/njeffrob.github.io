## Nicholas J. Roberts
![Self-portrait of N. J. Roberts]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/self_portrait-min.jpg "Nick's self-portrait")

BSc Honours, Dalhousie University (2023, Erin R. Johnson)

Nick graduated with a BSc Honours in Chemistry and Minor in Neuroscience from Dalhousie University in 2023, doing his honours with Prof. Erin R. Johnson. While at Dalhousie, Nick was heavily involved in research of all kinds, including main group inorganic synthetic chemistry, density-functional theory, and accessibility in chemical education.

Nick is currently an incoming Master's student at the University of Victoria for the McIndoe lab. There, he will be researching robotics/automation processes in chemistry. 

Nick has interests in all things chemistry and computers. If you would like to get into contact with Nick, please feel free to message him.
